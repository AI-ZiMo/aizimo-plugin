# Privacy Policy Questionnaire

Use this questionnaire to gather information about the app's data collection practices. Ask these questions after running the codebase scan to clarify and supplement automated findings.

## Basic Information

1. **App Name**: What is the official name of your application?
2. **App Type**: What type of application is this? (Mobile app, Web app, Desktop app, Browser extension)
3. **Company/Developer Name**: Who develops and operates this application?
4. **Contact Information**: What email address should users contact for privacy questions?
5. **Effective Date**: When should this privacy policy take effect?

## Data Collection

### Personal Information

6. **Account Information**: Do you collect email addresses, names, or usernames when users create accounts?
7. **Profile Data**: Do users provide additional profile information (age, gender, preferences, bio, etc.)?
8. **Contact Information**: Do you collect phone numbers, addresses, or other contact details?
9. **Payment Information**: Do you process credit cards or other payment methods? (If yes, note if you use a third-party processor)

### Automatically Collected Data

10. **Device Information**: Do you collect device type, OS version, or device identifiers?
11. **Usage Analytics**: Do you track how users interact with the app (features used, time spent, etc.)?
12. **Location Data**: Do you collect precise or approximate location? (GPS, IP-based, etc.)
13. **Diagnostic Data**: Do you collect crash reports, error logs, or performance data?
14. **Cookies**: Do you use cookies or similar tracking technologies?

### Content and Communications

15. **User Content**: Do users create, upload, or share content (photos, videos, text, etc.)?
16. **Messages**: Can users send messages to each other or to support?
17. **Voice/Camera**: Do you access microphone or camera?
18. **Contacts**: Do you access the user's contact list?

## Data Usage

19. **Primary Purpose**: What is the main reason you collect this data? (Provide service, improve features, etc.)
20. **Personalization**: Do you use data to personalize the user experience?
21. **Marketing**: Do you use data for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes?
22. **Analytics**: Do you analyze data to improve the app or understand user behavior?
23. **Security**: Do you use data for fraud prevention or security purposes?

## Data Sharing

24. **Third-Party Services**: Which third-party services does your app integrate with? (Analytics, payment processors, cloud hosting, etc.)
25. **Service Providers**: Do you share data with contractors or vendors who help operate the app?
26. **Business Transfers**: Should the policy mention that data may be transferred if the company is acquired?
27. **Legal Requirements**: Do you share data when required by law or to protect rights?
28. **User-Directed Sharing**: Can users share their data with others through the app?
29. **Advertising Partners**: Do you share data with advertisers or ad networks?

## Data Retention

30. **Retention Period**: How long do you keep user data? (Until account deletion, specific timeframe, etc.)
31. **Account Deletion**: Can users request deletion of their account and data?
32. **Backup Retention**: How long might data remain in backups after deletion?

## User Rights

33. **Access**: Can users request a copy of their personal data?
34. **Correction**: Can users update or correct their information?
35. **Deletion**: Can users request deletion of their data?
36. **Opt-Out**: Can users opt out of marketing communications or certain data collection?
37. **Export**: Can users export their data in a portable format?

## Security

38. **Encryption**: Do you encrypt data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest?
39. **Security Measures**: What specific security measures do you have in place?
40. **Data Breaches**: How would you notify users of a data breach?

## Special Considerations

41. **Children**: Is your app directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in EU)?
42. **Age Restrictions**: Do you have age restrictions? What is the minimum age?
43. **International Users**: Do you have users in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA)?
44. **Cross-Border Transfers**: Do you transfer data internationally?

## Compliance Requirements

45. **GDPR**: Do you need to comply with GDPR (EU users)?
46. **CCPA**: Do you need to comply with CCPA (California users)?
47. **COPPA**: Is your app directed at children or knowingly collect children's data?
48. **Other Regulations**: Are there industry-specific regulations you must comply with?

## Policy Updates

49. **Update Notification**: How will you notify users of privacy policy changes?
50. **Review Frequency**: How often do you plan to review and update this policy?
